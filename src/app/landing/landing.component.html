Medicine Made Easy

We want you to stay on top of your medication, so we built a one stop page for everything you need to know! With PrescribeMate, scheduling drugs has never been more simple.

Find and add new prescriptions

You’ll find any prescriptions from your doctor here! You will also be able to find and add any over-the-counter drugs you are taking to your list.

Your next dose

The big number on the next page tells you what your next med is and when to take it. You can’t miss it!

Find the bad stuff

We also list any side effects commonly associated with the drugs you may be using, so keep an eye out for those!

A dashboard for your medication

At PrescribeMate, we understand that it's hard to keep up with the copious amounts of medication given you by doctors. We want to make it easier for you to know what your medicine does!

PrescribeMate tells you when your next dose is, can reorder prescriptions automatically, and will give you the information you need to have a proper experience with medication.

Talk to your Doc

We show you the warning signs you need to be aware of so that you can effectively communicate to your doctor. One day, we hope to be your direct route of communication with your doctor!
